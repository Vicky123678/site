With a super annual output of 13.5 million ㎡, Xusheng Electronics leads the whole PCB industry in its production capacity

     As an important electronic connection, PCB is known as the "mother of electronic products" reputation, almost all electronic products are inseparable from PCB.

　　 

　　

　　 

　　Since 2017, with the continuous development of electronic information technology, the total output value of the global PCB industry has shown an obvious recovery phenomenon. According to analysts, AI-related memory, sensors and basic communication hardware devices could be the next breakout point in 2018, when global PCB production is expected to exceed $55 billion again.

　　 

　　In such a large market space, Xusheng Electronics promotes innovation with science and technology, relies on the scale, production line and other advantages, in the PCB industry with an annual output of 13.5 million square meters of super capacity, a standout, become the industry leader. The PCB manufactured by Xusheng Electronics is widely used in medical equipment, automotive electronics, consumer electronics, smart home, 5G communication and control systems and other fields.

　　 

　　Xusheng Electronics Phase I Capacity: 13.5 million m²

　　Xusheng Electronics, founded in 2017, has a total investment of RMB 2 billion. The first phase of the company covers an area of 267 thousand ㎡, with a construction area of 320,000 m², and has four plants (four floors).

　　

　　The first phase has 4 plants with an area of 320,000 m²

　　 

　　With the world's longest linear intelligent integrated production line in PCB industry -- a production line of 460 meters long, the total production capacity of the FR-4 single and double layers pcb produced by Xusheng Electronics can reach 1.12 million m²/ month, and 13.5 million m² single and double layers pcb can be produced annually. In addition, according to the expectation, Xusheng Electronics is expected to start the second phase of production in 2020, with the goal of reaching an annual production capacity of 27 million m².

　　Compared with the PCB industry's annual production capacity of 100,000 m², millions of m², whether it is compared with foreign PCB manufacturers, or with other leading domestic PCB manufacturers, Xusheng's annual production capacity of 13.5 million m² is very amazing.

　　 

　　High degree of automation of production line is the cornerstone of super capacity 

　　In the fierce industry competition, Xusheng Electronics seeks development by innovation. The annual production capacity of 13.5 million m² is greatly benefited from the R&D innovation and equipment investment in the early stage.

　　 

　　

　　 

　　Every year, the company invests more than 10 million for independent research and development of new production equipment, most of the existing production equipment are in accordance with the HDI standard production equipment independent research and development.

　　It is planned to build 16 intelligent and integrated PCB automatic production lines of 460 meters in the 4 plants put into production in the first phase to realize highly automated production. In the process of automatic production line operation, the products will not be off the production line after drilling until the packaging board, so that the employees can't touch the products. The production process of the products is free from human interference, which ensures the quality of the products far higher than the IPC standard.

　　 

　　

　　Xusheng electronic production line is highly automated

　　 

　　At the same time, through innovation, the labor cost is also reduced to the limit. The production line with a production capacity of 5000 people only needs more than 400 people after the highly automatic integrated production line, and the unmanned workshop management of Industry 4.0 is realized in advance.

　　 

　　Capacity upgrade, PCB industry leader

　　According to the current industry trend, PCB production capacity moves eastward, and the proportion of China's output value continues to increase. The demand of domestic PCB market keeps growing, and the concentration degree of downstream domestic customers such as domestic communication, consumer electronics and automotive electronics is also increasing, which also puts forward higher requirements for the quality of PCB products. The expansion of production by large factories will be the general trend.

　　 

　　

　　 

　　It goes without saying that the current industry situation is a very good opportunity for Xusheng Electronics. With a plant size of 320,000 m² in the first phase and an annual capacity of 13.5 million m², Xusheng Electronics can firmly occupy a position in the industry. After the first phase of production, the second phase will continue to expand the production capacity, so that the company's production capacity can continue to meet the growing production demand of the industry. "Follow the trend, move with the trend", the future of Xusheng Electronics will have a bright future!

　　At the same time, Xusheng Electronics actively respond to national policies, adhere to innovation, capacity upgrading, constantly enhance competitiveness, improve product quality, optimize the industrial chain, promote the healthy development of the industry, will be able to become a real leading force in the PCB industry.
