Xusheng Electronics -- "Future Star" in PCB Industry

　　Jiangxi Xusheng Electronics Co., Ltd. is a joint-stock private enterprise, established in 2017, with a total investment of RMB 2 billion. The company is located in Chengxi Industrial Park, Jishui County, Ji 'an City, Jiangxi Province, mainly engaged in PCB manufacturing business. Its products are widely used in medical equipment, automotive electronics, consumer electronics, smart home, 5G communication and control systems and other fields.

　　

　　As a rookie in the industry, compared with many old established enterprises in the industry, Xusheng Electronics appears to be very young. However, Xusheng Electronics has three core competencies: scale advantage, automated production line and high cost performance. It has amazing strength in industry position, innovation, growth and development potential.

　　 

　　The industry scale is leading in the world

　　The first phase of the company covers an area of 267 thousand ㎡, plant area of 320 thousand ㎡, mainly produce FR-4 single and double layers (except LED display P series board), the total production capacity of 1.12 million ㎡/ month, annual production capacity of nearly 13.5 million ㎡. Our company is the leading PCB manufacturer in the industry.

　　

　　With large output, high technology and concentrated production, Xusheng Electronics will gradually become the backbone enterprise of PCB production. With the development of downstream PCB application 5G communication, Xuseng Electronics will also play a leading role in the future industry development and lead the new trend of 5G development by relying on its own scale and capacity advantages.

　　 

　　The world's longest automatic production line

　　Xusheng Electronics has the world's advanced PCB production line, the first phase have 16 production lines. The production line is 460 meters long, which is the world's longest linear intelligent integration production line in PCB industry.

　　The company adopts the intensive production mode, the production line is highly automated and intelligent. Automatic connection design, reduce manual operation, improve unit output, the timeliness of production is also strongly guaranteed. Imported into MES system to achieve intelligent control. Automatic product identification, automatic parameter setting, reduce the operator to the error of parameter setting operation. Production of products to achieve real-time record progress, real-time monitoring equipment situation.

　　

　　In the process of automatic production line operation, the products will not be off the production line after drilling process until the packaging board, so that the employees can't touch the boards. The production process of the products is free from human interference, which ensures the quality of the products far higher than the IPC standard.

　　 

　　The product cost performance leads the industry

　　Xusheng PCB production strictly in accordance with the strict standards of "fast, high, quality, guarantee" (namely fast delivery, high cost performance, industry-leading quality, after-sales guarantee), fully meet customer needs, is committed to providing customers with more than expected product value, to achieve industry-leading cost performance.

　　Due to the use of advanced equipment, the use of unique technical advantages, Xusheng electronics have higher efficiency and quality than the traditional factory's single and double boards in production. Therefore, on the basis of mass production, Xusheng Electronics can make small profits and quick turnover, so as to achieve the best market quality and the lowest price compared to other factories.

　　 

　　

　　 

　　The company has independent research and development design of special equipment and production process.

　　The production equipment of Xusheng Electronics includes the new 6 axis motor drilling machine, the new electroplating line, the new copper immersed wire, the vacuum etching line (etching capacity of 0.05/0.05mm), and the new intelligent production line which is independently developed and owned by Xusheng Electronics.

　　In terms of technology, the company pays attention to the strategy of intellectual property rights. At the beginning of its establishment, the company began to apply for patents, and the number of patents applied exceeded 120 projects in half a year.

　　At the same time, the intelligent production also reduces the labor cost to the limit. The production line with the capacity of 5000 people needs only 400 people after the highly automatic integrated production line, so as to realize the unmanned workshop management of Industry 4.0 in advance.

　　 

　　

　　 

　　Xusheng electronic has a pioneering and enterprising, respect science, have technical knowledge and management skills, good at developing the market, honest and trustworthy, to customer requirements for their own duty, fully develop the team spirit of the staff team, since its establish, the team has continued to study, strive for safety, strive for the best service.

　　

　　 

　　Driven by the vigorous development of PCB industry, Jiangxi Xusheng Electronics will also usher in a golden period of development. With the world's leading industrial scale (annual production capacity of 13.5 million m²), the world's longest intelligent production line (reach a length of 460m), In the future, Xuseng Electronics is expected to become the world's leading medium and high-end single and double boards manufacturer, the authority and industry standard setting supplier of PCB industry, and provide customers with the most cost-effective PCB production services.
