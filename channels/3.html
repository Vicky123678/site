    Jiangxi Xusheng electronic Co., Ltd is a collection research and development production and sales in the integration of PCB manufacturing enterprises, the company formerly known as Dongguan jiu jing electronics Co., Ltd., was founded in 2010, the main business is PCB manufacturing business, through 8 years of precipitation and the development of industry, the quality of the jiu jing electronic delivery service, price system has won good praise of the industry research and development of production scale rapidly expanding. In order to establish the centralized industrial development scale, better service to customers, in November 2017, Jiangxi Xusheng Electronic Co., Ltd was set up. Xusheng electronices have scale advantage, automatic production line and high cost performance these three core competitiveness. Developed so far, in the industry position, innovative growth, development potential and other aspects of amazing strength.

    Jiangxi Xusheng Electronics Co., Ltd is located in Jishui County, Jiangxi Province, with a total investment of more than 2 billion yuan, a plant area of more than 320,000 square meters, and a total production capacity of 1.12 million square meters per month. Our company pays attention to the establishment of long-term strategic cooperation relationship with customers, in line with the purpose of "people-oriented, customer first", to provide customers with the best quality service and cost-effective products, is committed to become the authority of PCB industry and industry standard setting supplier.
